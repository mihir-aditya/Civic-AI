# NagarRakshak - Civic AI

### Report. Alert. Prevent.

An open-source, AI-powered community safety platform that helps citizens report public hazards, receive real-time alerts, and contribute towards safer cities.

---

## Why NagarRakshak?

Every year, thousands of accidents occur across India due to:

* Potholes
* Open drains
* Open manholes
* Waterlogging
* Broken streetlights
* Damaged roads
* Construction hazards
* Poorly maintained public infrastructure

Many of these issues remain unresolved for weeks or months, while citizens have no reliable way to report, track, or receive warnings about dangerous locations.

NagarRakshak aims to bridge this gap by combining community participation, location intelligence, and AI-powered hazard detection.

---

## Our Mission

To create a community-driven platform where citizens can identify, report, verify, track, and receive alerts about public hazards, helping prevent accidents before they happen.

---

## Core Features

### Hazard Reporting

Citizens can report:

* Potholes
* Open Drains
* Open Manholes
* Waterlogging
* Broken Streetlights
* Garbage Accumulation
* Damaged Roads
* Other Public Hazards

Each report includes:

* Photo
* GPS Location
* Description
* Timestamp

---

### AI-Powered Hazard Detection

Using Google Gemini Vision:

* Detect hazard type automatically
* Suggest category
* Estimate severity
* Generate issue summary

Example:

Hazard Type: Open Drain

Severity: High

Confidence: 92%

---

### Hazard Map

Interactive map displaying:

🟢 Low Risk Areas

🟡 Medium Risk Areas

🔴 High Risk Areas

Users can:

* View hazards near them
* Search locations
* Filter by category
* Filter by severity

---

### Community Verification

Community members can:

* Verify reports
* Add evidence
* Confirm resolutions
* Flag incorrect reports

This helps improve report accuracy and trust.

---

### Hazard Alerts

One of the platform's most important features.

Users receive alerts when approaching dangerous locations.

Examples:

⚠ Open Drain Ahead

⚠ Severe Pothole 150m Away

⚠ Waterlogged Road Ahead

Alert Methods:

* Push Notifications
* Voice Alerts
* In-App Notifications

---

### Issue Tracking

Every issue follows a lifecycle:

Reported

→ Verified

→ Escalated

→ In Progress

→ Resolved

Citizens can monitor progress transparently.
