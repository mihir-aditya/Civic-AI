# Android App UI/UX Design Prompt

Design a modern, premium, production-ready Android application called **NagarRakshak** (Community Safety & Hazard Alert Platform).

The app's mission is to help citizens report public hazards, receive alerts about dangerous locations, verify community reports, and make cities safer.

## Design Style

* Modern Material 3 Design
* Premium, clean, minimalist interface
* Green and white color palette
* High-quality shadows and elevation
* Rounded corners (16px-24px)
* Smooth micro-interactions
* Google Maps inspired elements
* Mobile-first UX
* Accessibility friendly
* Native Android feel
* Professional government-tech startup appearance
